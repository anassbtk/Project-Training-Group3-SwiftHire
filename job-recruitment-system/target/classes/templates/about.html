About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2025, SwiftHire was born from a simple idea: the job search process should be easier, faster, and more human.

Our founders, a group of Moroccan students from Dalian Polytechnic University, saw firsthand how outdated platforms and clunky interfaces were failing both job seekers and employers. Talented candidates were lost in a "black hole" of applications, while companies struggled to find the right fit.

We set out to build something better. A platform that leverages smart technology to make meaningful connections, not just match keywords.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower careers and build great teams by connecting the right talent with the right opportunity, seamlessly and efficiently.

  • For Job Seekers: To provide a transparent, supportive, and intuitive platform to find jobs that match your skills and ambitions.
  • For Employers: To deliver a powerful, streamlined hiring tool that connects you with qualified, engaged candidates ready to make an impact.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide us every day.

Connection
